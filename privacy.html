Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how LumaDent Studio collects, uses and protects information when you visit our website or contact our clinic


Effective Date: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

LumaDent Studio respects your privacy. We only collect information that helps us respond to enquiries, manage appointment requests and improve the experience of visitors using our website.

This website is designed as an informational dental clinic website. We do not sell personal information or create customer accounts through this website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information when you complete a contact form, booking enquiry or communicate with us directly.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Preferred appointment date or time
  • Treatment or enquiry type
  • Any message or details you choose to send us

2.2 Website Information

We may collect basic website usage information such as browser type, device type, pages visited and general performance data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for practical clinic and customer service purposes, including:

  • Responding to contact enquiries
  • Managing booking requests
  • Confirming appointment details
  • Answering questions about treatments, pricing or opening hours
  • Improving our website and patient experience

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We may only share information where necessary to operate our website, respond to your enquiry, comply with legal obligations or protect our business.

5. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and only retain enquiry details for as long as needed to respond to your request or manage clinic communication.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct or request deletion of personal information we hold about you.

To make a privacy-related request, please contact us using the details on our contact page.

7. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies or similar technologies to improve performance, remember preferences and understand general website usage.

For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

8. Updates To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
