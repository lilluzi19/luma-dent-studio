Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how LumaDent Studio may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website


Effective Date: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how LumaDent Studio uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

Cookies help websites function properly, remember preferences and understand general visitor behaviour.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your browser and support features such as preferences, security and performance.

2.1 Similar Technologies

We may also use similar technologies such as local storage, session storage or embedded third-party tools that help the website operate correctly.

3. Types Of Cookies We May Use

Our website may use a small number of cookies or similar technologies for practical website purposes, including:

  • Essential cookies needed for the website to work
  • Preference cookies that remember choices such as theme settings
  • Performance cookies that help us understand general website usage
  • Security cookies that help protect forms and website features
  • Third-party cookies from embedded services such as maps

4. Why We Use Cookies

Cookies help us provide a smoother and more reliable website experience for visitors.

  • Keeping the website secure and functional
  • Remembering display preferences
  • Improving page speed and performance
  • Understanding which pages are most useful to visitors
  • Supporting embedded tools such as location maps

5. Third-Party Services

Some pages may include third-party services, such as embedded maps, social links or external content. These services may place their own cookies or collect usage information according to their own policies.

We do not control third-party cookies and recommend reviewing the relevant third-party privacy or cookie policies where appropriate.

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage or block cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to delete cookies, block cookies or receive alerts before cookies are stored.

Please note that blocking some cookies may affect how certain website features work.

7. Local Storage & Preferences

Our website may use local storage to remember simple preferences, such as light or dark mode. This helps maintain a consistent experience when you return to the website.

Local storage is stored in your browser and can usually be cleared through your browser settings.

8. Updates To This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
